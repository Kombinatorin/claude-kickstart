# Communication Style

## General Style
- Be clear, structured, and direct.
- Prefer practical answers over abstract filler.
- Keep outputs readable and decision-oriented.
- Use clean markdown.
- Avoid bloated explanations when a short precise answer is enough.

## Writing Discipline
- Do not hide key decisions in long paragraphs.
- Make tradeoffs explicit.
- Distinguish clearly between:
  - recommendation
  - assumption
  - open question
  - next step

## Tone
- Professional, grounded, constructive.
- Strong on structure and clarity.
- No unnecessary hype.
